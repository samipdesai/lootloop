LootLoop turns everyday chores into something the whole family looks forward to. Parents set up chores and rewards; kids complete tasks, earn points, save up, and cash in for rewards you choose together.

For parents:
• Create chores with point values — one-off or recurring
• Approve completed chores and award points in a tap
• Build a reward store your kids actually want
• Manage multiple kids from one account

For kids:
• See today's chores and check them off
• Earn points and watch savings grow
• Spend points in the family reward store
• Build daily reading streaks
• A friendly, age-appropriate experience on iPhone & iPad

LootLoop helps kids learn responsibility, patience, and the value of earning — wrapped in a playful experience they'll want to come back to. No ads.
